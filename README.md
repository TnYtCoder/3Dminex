## ✨ What you can do

- **Upload any model** – `.glb`, `.gltf`, `.obj`, `.stl` (up to 100 MB)
- **Drop an HDRI** – use `.hdr` files for realistic lighting
- **Move, rotate, scale** – full control with simple sliders
- **Tweak lights** – change colours and brightness of ambient + directional lights
- **Show/hide helpers** – grid, axes, wireframe mode
- **Reset anytime** – one click to reset camera or model
- **Drag & drop** – quick and easy
- **Works everywhere** – desktop, tablet, mobile

## 🎮 How to use it

- **Load a model** – click the upload box or drag a file in. The model appears centred, ready to view.
- **Add an HDRI** – upload an `.hdr` file to change the background and reflections. Click *clear HDRI* to go back to grey.
- **Move it around** – use the X, Y, Z sliders under **Transform**.
- **Rotate** – the Rot X/Y/Z sliders turn the model (values are in degrees).
- **Scale** – make it bigger or smaller with the Scale slider.
- **Change lighting** – pick colours and adjust brightness for ambient and two directional lights.
- **Grid, axes, wireframe** – check/uncheck the boxes to show or hide them.
- **Reset** – *Reset camera* brings your view back to start; *Reset model* puts the model back at origin.
